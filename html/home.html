Find answers to common questions about VaxCare

VaxCare is a website that is designed to help you keep track of all your vaccinations in many ways.
First, you can take a test to know which vaccine you have and need to be up-to-date.
Then, we provide reminders to help you not miss any shots.
And finally, we help you plan your travels thanks to our database of vaccines and their requirements in each country.

Your privacy is our priority. All your data is stored exclusively on your device and is never shared with anyone.
We do not have access to your data nor do we store it on our servers. This way, you can be sure that your data is safe and secure.

Our information is constantly updated to ensure that you have the most accurate information possible.
We rely on official governements sources to provide you with the most accurate information like sante.fr in France.
However, if you notice any inaccuracies, please let us know so that we can correct them as soon as possible.
